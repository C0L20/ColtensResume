About Me

Hello! My name is Colten Brass! and I am proud to be from the First Nations Treaty 6 Territory. I have a passion for both my personal and professional life, always striving to grow and advance in my interests. I enjoy outings that revolve around food, with sushi, BBQ, and hot pot being some of my favorites. In my leisure time, I love immersing myself in video games, often sharing these experiences with friends.

My enthusiasm for gaming extends beyond just playing; I am deeply interested in building video games and exploring the capabilities of Unreal Engine. Additionally, I am dedicated to enhancing my skills in web development, continually learning and experimenting with new technologies.

I am engaged to a wonderful woman who supports and inspires me every day. We've been together for 5 happy years and counting! It helps that we have combines interests in video games, television and movies! We share our home with two beloved cats who bring joy and companionship to our lives.

I believe that the future holds incredible opportunities, and I am particularly excited about the advancements in technology that we are witnessing today. This excitement fuels my drive to stay at the forefront of technological progress and to integrate it into both my hobbies and career. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me.
